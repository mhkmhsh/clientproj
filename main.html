About Sutapa Pal


I am an International Award Winning (PLFPOTY 2023) Lifestyle photographer working as a freelancer since last 6 years with specialization in Food and Beverage, Interior & Architecture, Portraiture, Travel and Lifestyle Photography. Have done projects for 200+ brands and start-ups. I was a photo enthusiast and pursued photography as a hobby while working in corporate sector for 15 years. Before being a photographer, I was a banker. Started my career with American Express, Barclays and lastly The Royal Bank of Scotland when in 2018, I resigned as an Associate Vice President and went to The One School Goa to learn creative photography which honed my skills as a photographer.

Beyond client work, I love travelling for my personal photography projects and also mentor aspiring photographers, conducting workshops at the IIP Noida, Genesis School, GOA Arts Academy and corporate organizations like ISGEC.
